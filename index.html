Lajes Field, Azores, Portugal

My first assignment was quite a surprise. The Azores is an archipelago of nine islands located in the north Atlantic ocean off the coast of Portugal.

McGuire AFB Wrightstown, NJ

Next stop landed me on the East Coast in the small town of Wrightstown, NJ. Although I was assigned at McGuire AFB, I spent most of my time on the road. I was a Contingency Communications Radio Operator which meant where there was a war or possible contingency, my unit was there. Of course, back in the '90s they claim women didn't go to war.
